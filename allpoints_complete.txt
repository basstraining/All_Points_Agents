## ğŸ“‹ Demo Overview

### 1. **Carrier Exception Monitor** 
- **Pain Point**: AEs manually check tracking for 1000+ shipments daily
- **Solution**: Auto-detect delays, draft customer emails, alert AEs
- **ROI**: Save 12.5 hours/day across team
- **Systems**: ShipStation, UPS/FedEx APIs, Outlook, Power BI

### 2. **Takt Labor Profitability Analyzer**
- **Pain Point**: "Which clients are profitable? Where are we bleeding money?"
- **Solution**: Combine Takt labor data + QB revenue â†’ profitability reports
- **ROI**: Identify $60K/year in repricing opportunities
- **Systems**: Takt LMS, QuickBooks Online, Excel, Power BI

### 3. **ShipStation Rate Shopping Optimizer**
- **Pain Point**: Manual rate shopping between UPS accounts on every order
- **Solution**: Auto-compare rates, select cheapest, generate label
- **ROI**: $130K/year in shipping cost savings
- **Systems**: ShipStation (2 accounts), Power BI

### 4. **RetailReady Chargeback Defender**
- **Pain Point**: $10-50K/month in retail compliance chargebacks
- **Solution**: Auto-compile evidence, draft dispute letters, track resolution
- **ROI**: $35K/year additional chargebacks won
- **Systems**: RetailReady, Outlook, OneDrive, QuickBooks

### 5. **MyCarrier LTL Automation**
- **Pain Point**: 15-20 min per freight quote (3-5 daily)
- **Solution**: Auto-quote from 5+ carriers, email customer, auto-book
- **ROI**: $15K time savings + $30K margin improvement
- **Systems**: MyCarrier TMS, Outlook, QuickBooks

### 6. **Front Email Revival** (Bonus)
- **Pain Point**: Failed Front implementation, AEs back on Outlook
- **Solution**: Auto-triage emails, 40% auto-respond, organized AE queues
- **ROI**: Justify $2,400 Front investment + save 4 hrs/day
- **Systems**: Front, ShipStation, Outlook, Power BI
